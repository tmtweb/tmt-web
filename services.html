Transcription and Subtitle Redaction services

You might have some old videos or audio tracks left as a bequest that would require some translation or transcription. We offer that service too! Every video or audio file can be transcribed in our offices and sent back to you. We will work hard to make sure the assignment is genuine and easy to understand. Old English, as other languages, might have some different dialects and we always make sure of using the rights terms and explain them if needed.

Of course, our Transcription service can be ordered with a document translation. This way, you will be able to understand the video or the interview in a foreign language that has the key elements for your Master’s Thesis. One can order subtitles to go with their video for their family and friends to watch, all together, the newlyweds’ celebration.

*Please note that TranslateMEthis.com does not provide any notaries’ certification, only good quality translations.

H.R Assistance

One of our main objectives with our translation service is to help get the world closer to each other. Many of our employees had to move to a different country in their life. Just like you, they were planning on travelling or moving out from their native cities. One thing was always missing, H.R assistance in their new Host-Country. TranslateMEthis.com offers some Human Resource Assistance to those who plan on travelling to other countries and are looking for a job seeking help. We offer Resume, cover letters and follow-up emails translation and adaptation to your host-country’s culture and etiquette.

Alternatively, we also offer help with your job search. We will ask our staff to post your resume for you on the main job search websites of your host-country. We will even apply for you.* So that when you finally land in your new home, you already have a good place to work at!

*All the latest services are subject to different prices, relatively on the location and the domain.

Interpreter locator service

Our interpreter locator platform is truly an innovative service. We offer our contact network, to those who need it. Whether it is for business, for a personal event or celebration or whether it is simply to visit the city. Our collaborators will be there in your host country and help you get around. They will take care of you like if you were family.

Interpreters, guides and other collaborators that are offering this service are all very professional and friendly. You will, of course, be introduced to our contact beforehand. Also, do not worry about your safety, since we specifically ask our staff to provide us with criminal records and other personal information before they can be among our list of interpreters and guides.

*Fees are applied for the contact information only (if available). The hourly wage of the collaborator has to be determined by a mutual agreement.
