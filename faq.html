Where are you located?

We often get this question. Our offices are located in Quebec City, Canada. However, our freelance translators, transcriptors and other collaborators are free agents spread all around the world. Therefore, we can say that TranslateMEthis.com is located everywhere! And that’s our strength.

How long will it take for my order to be processed?

We strive to provide an honest translation service. When you place an order on our website, you can choose the time-frame at the same time you process your payment. We will do the extra mile and work very hard to meet your needs. We know how this is important for you and that is why we strive to deliver all assignments on time. In the rate cases when our collaborator will need more time, you will be the first informed, and compensation will surely be applied on your next order.

Why does the price changes with the urgency?

When you place an order on our website, you may choose the deadline for your assignment. Of course, this time frame includes the following services, the selection of our translator/transcription, the redaction, and the QA control. The more urgent the paper, the more expensive it will be to motivate the team do its best for your own good!

Can I use another Payment processing company?

So far, only PayPal ™ is available on our website.

I am a translator/interpreter/transcriber/language professional. Do you hire?

Yes, we are constantly looking for new translators, trancribers and H.R collaborators. You may send your resume and cover letter to our HR department at hr@translatemethis.com.

Is this legal?

It certainly is. You are right to wonder since in some countries translators and other language specialists should be members of an order. However, TranslateMEthis.com offers its services for common translations and transcriptions only. This means that we will not provide any notaries certifications on the translated documents. However, we always provide high quality translated documents and we follow the standards of the industry.

Why do I have to pay taxes? .

Every product you buy has a price. We are really working hard to keep our services cheap. Still., taxes are inevitable. Taxes are determined by your location.
